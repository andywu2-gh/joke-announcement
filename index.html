Cisco Announces Mandatory Global Return to Office — Five Days Per Week, Effective June 2, 2025

April 1, 2025 By Cisco People & Communities 5 min read
↗ ✉ 🔖
🏢

Cisco is announcing a significant update to our hybrid work policy. Beginning Monday, June 2, 2025, all employees globally will be required to work from a Cisco office location five days per week. This decision was made following an extensive review by the Executive Leadership Team and reflects our commitment to driving collaboration, innovation, and cultural connection across the company.

Key Date Full five-day, in-office attendance is required for all employees beginning June 2, 2025. Managers will communicate office assignments by May 1, 2025.

A Message from Our CEO

"The energy and creativity that comes from being together in person is simply irreplaceable," said Chuck Robbins, Chair and CEO. "After careful consideration and listening to our customers, our board, and the data from our own Webex Insights platform, we are confident that a return to full in-office work is the right move for Cisco's next chapter. We are investing significantly in our campuses to make them the best places our employees have ever worked."

What This Means for You

Effective June 2, 2025, all Cisco employees will be expected to report to their assigned office location five days per week, Monday through Friday. The following guidelines apply:

  • Employees must badge into a Cisco facility each working day to confirm attendance.
  • Remote-only roles created between 2020 and 2024 will be converted to office-based roles.
  • Employees who relocated more than 50 miles from a Cisco office during the pandemic will be provided relocation assistance to return within commuting distance.
  • Exceptions for medical accommodations will continue to be handled through Global Benefits.
  • Contractor and vendor access policies will be updated separately by Procurement.

Office Investments & Amenities

Cisco is investing over $1.2 billion in campus improvements globally in anticipation of this change. Highlights include expanded café and wellness spaces, upgraded collaboration zones with the latest Webex Room Kit technology, on-site childcare at 14 additional campuses, and complimentary daily breakfast at all major sites.

Commuter Benefits Update Monthly commuter allowances will increase to $350 USD (or local equivalent) to help offset transportation costs. Pre-tax commuter accounts will be automatically topped up beginning May 1.

Frequently Asked Questions

A comprehensive FAQ will be published on the Employee Connection by April 15. Your manager will also receive a detailed briefing guide this week. Town halls with the People & Communities team will be held the weeks of April 14 and April 21 — times and Webex links will be distributed via email and posted in the #workplace-updates space on Webex Teams.

Next Steps

  • Watch for an email from your VP or SVP with location-specific details by April 8.
  • Update your office location in Workday by May 1, 2025.
  • Reach out to your HR Business Partner with questions or accommodation requests.
  • Review the updated Travel & Expense policy on the Finance portal.

We recognize this is a significant change and we are committed to supporting you through the transition. Thank you for everything you do every day to make Cisco the company our customers and partners rely on.
